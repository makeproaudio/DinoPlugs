Basic installation

To install the UAC plugin in your NSIS setup, you just need to copy two files from the above ZIP:

    For ANSI installers, copy UAH.nsh into your Include directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\NSIS\Include) and UAC.dll from Release/A into your Plugins directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\NSIS\Plugins).

    For Unicode installers, copy UAH.nsh into your Include directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\NSIS\Unicode\Include) and UAC.dll from Release/U nto your Plugins directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\NSIS\Unicode\Plugins).
